Who am I?

To preserve anonymity, I’m going to refrain from providing any personal details about my life, but I’d like to give you a rough idea of whose sitting behind the screen.

I’m currently a student at York University in Toronto. My interests are wildly varied and tend to fluctuate day by day, sometimes by the hour. I built this website and will continue to study computer science (my current area of focus is JavaScript), but I also fill my days with philosophy, politics, psychology, neuroscience, social science, chess, and I love to bake.

I have a diploma in performance, (soon to have) a bachelors degree in psychology, with a concentration on neuropsychology, and hopefully at some point in the next few years, a masters/PhD - however I do have many qualms with post secondary institutions, so maybe not.
